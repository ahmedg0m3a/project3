Popular destinations in Egypt


Giza is considered one of the largest tourist trips in Egypt as it is rich in Egyptian antiquities, such as the Giza Pyramids and the Great Sphinx. Hotel options and bazaars. The Dinosaur Park, the first incubator park in Africa, is located in Giza.

A controversial tourism development plan aims to transform Luxor into the biggest vast open-air museum. The master plan envisions new roads, five-star hotels, glitzy shops, and an IMAX theatre. The main attraction is an 11 million dollar project to unearth and restore the 2.7 kilometres (1.7 miles) long Avenue of Sphinxes that once linked Luxor and Karnak temples.

Sinai is distinguished by the fact that it witnessed the most important religious events in the three heavenly religions, Judaism, Christianity, and Islam, such as the exodus of the children of Israel from Egypt, the journey of the Holy Family to Egypt, and the Islamic conquest of Egypt, which made Sinai important in the religions to the point that it was mentioned in the three heavenly books (the Torah, The Bible, the Holy Qur’an).
